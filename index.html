Pandemic is a game designed by Matt Leacock in 2008. Pandemic is based on the premise that four diseases have broken out in the world, each threatening to wipe out a region. The game accommodates two to four players, each playing one of seven possible roles: dispatcher, medic, scientist, researcher, operations expert, contingency planner or quarantine specialist. Through the combined effort of all the players, the goal is to discover all four cures before any of several game-losing conditions are reached.

How to play Scheherazade and the Sultan

You are Scheherazade and your friend is the Sultan.
Scheherazade’s goal is to narrate one fake incident and one true incident that they have witnessed in their life.
The Sultan’s aim is to listen to Scheherazade and to identify which of the two incidents is false.
If the Sultan succeeds, the sequence repeats and Scheherazade has to come up with a new pair of incidents.
If the Sultan fails to identify correctly, the roles are reversed - Scheherazade becomes the Sultan, and the Sultan becomes Scheherazade.
